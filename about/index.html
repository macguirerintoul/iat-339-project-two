The Moloch Story

John Stewart, better known by his stage name Moloch, is a Canadian electronic musician from Vancouver, British Columbia. He is a founding member of the electronic music collective 999DeathKult. Moloch released his debut album, Pfizer is a Girl's Best Friend, on 999DeathKult in 2011. His Donald trump EP was released in 2012. He released the Gold Chainz EP in 2013.

After Moloch remixed Weird Nite's "Pull Up" in 2015, the two worked together on a track titled "Rentboys" which was part of ALTOIDS / RENTBOYS and Adidas Originals' 2013 Songs from Scratch series. A collaborative EP with Günz was released on 999DeathKult in 2014.

On February 13, 2014, Moloch and Jeremih released a new song off their forthcoming EP together. The track, "No More", was premiered by NPR and was released in time for Valentine's Day.

On January 2, 2014, it was reported that rapper Bones was in talks to collaborate with Moloch, and on March 6, 2015, it was announced on Moloch's Twitter that they would play a show together at The Fonda Theatre on May 7, 2015.

Moloch announced via his Twitter account on February 7, 2018, that his third studio album will be titled “The End”.
